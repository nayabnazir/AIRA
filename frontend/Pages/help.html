User Guide

How to Use AIRA

Use these steps when you are new to the system.

1

Create or login to your account

Signup first, then login so AIRA can save your generated work in My History.

2

Generate SRS

Open Generate SRS, enter a project title and idea, or upload a source file, then generate the document.

3

Generate UML

Open Generate UML, describe your system, choose a diagram type, then edit the diagram if needed.

4

Check SRS quality

Open AI Analysis, paste or upload SRS text, and review ambiguity or improvement suggestions.

5

Describe UML images

Open UML Image Description, upload a UML image, and AIRA will extract and explain the diagram.

6

Reuse saved work

Open My History to view previous activity and reuse generated content in another tool.
